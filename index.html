Takaya U.

My name is Takaya and I am currently a third year university student at the University of Sydney and I have an interest in pure mathematics — particularly in areas such as abstract algebra, topology and analysis. As accessible here, I enjoy writing mathematical expositions that explore the wonders of mathematical concepts alongside its historical development.

Outside of mathematics, I spend much of my time reading and studying philosophy — especially works in logic and ethics — and exploring the writings of impportant figures from Plato, to Descartes to even those in the modern age such as Peter Singer. I also enjoy reading fiction; one of my favorites authors being Agatha Christie. Moreover, I spend most of my nights playing video games and listening to music. This is where I share my ongoing projects, writings, occasional guides and really anything I find interesting to talk about.
