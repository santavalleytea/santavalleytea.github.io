About Me

I am a Singapore-born Japanese currently in my third year undergraduate degree studying mathematics at the University of Sydney.

My interest lies in pure mathematics, specifically in understanding algebraic and topological spaces.

My goal is to eventually conduct research in mathematics and be able to help people understand the beauty and complexities of mathematics in an accessible manner.

Interests

My main interests lie in pure mathematics, particularly in abstract algebra and topology, where I enjoy exploring how algebraic structures and geometric intuition interact. I’m also passionate about mathematics education — finding clear and creative ways to communicate complex ideas through writing, visualization, and teaching.

Beyond pure math, I have a growing interest in cybersecurity, especially in defensive security and how mathematical reasoning and systems thinking contribute to building secure architectures.

Hobbies
