Takaya U.

My name is Takaya and I am currently an honours student in pure mathematics at the University of Sydney.

My current honours thesis, supervised by Professor Oded Yacobi and Postdoctoral Fellow Edmund Heng, concerns the combinatorics of finite Coxeter groups. I am mainly working towards showing that the class of Mikado braids coincides with the class of one-step pregalleries; a problem that draws on the geometric representation of finite Coxeter groups and some ideas from algebraic topology, particularly on fundamental groupoids.

Broadly speaking, I am interested in group theory and algebraic topology: the way in which groups encode symmetry, and the way tools such as the fundamental group reveals hidden structures of particular spaces.
